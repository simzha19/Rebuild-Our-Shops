Blossom Shoes

Shoe Store
Chicago, IL

Like many immigrant families, my first generation Korean parents have worked through blood, sweat, and tears to build this proud business of their own in hopes to support a better life for my siblings and me. My parents have worked 365 days a year for as long as I can remember and have never taken off for holidays. Over the years, they have grown deeply fond of the customers in the community with love that’s close to family. They’ve worked long enough to even witness multiple generations of family come in to buy newborn shoes, back to school shoes, new-job shoes and even birthday shoes. They have grown up with the community.

Santa Monica Music Center

Music Shop
Los Angeles, CA

The music store and music studios have been providing the music community, schools, and local families with quality band and orchestra instrument sales, rentals, repair, private and group lessons and studio rentals since 1972. The community minded business supports the public and private schools in the area with donations of lessons and instruments for their auctions and fundraisers.
