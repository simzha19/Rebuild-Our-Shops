To our visitors,

We thank you for checking out our website. We absolutely stand with the black community and we demand justice for George Floyd, the latest victim in a long history of police brutality. However, we also wanted to support the locally owned businesses that have suffered from rioting and looting. We created this site in order to list locally owned businesses, bring attention to their fundraising efforts, as well as give users a place to find companies that have been looted after the protests and pandemic is over. We hope you’re able to find places you’ve otherwise never heard of before.

If you are an owner of a locally owned business, please fill out this form here if you wish to be listed on our website. We wish you all a speedy recovery.

Sincerely,

The Founders of Rebuild Our Shops.
